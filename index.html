Can you guess which artist has more monthly listeners on Spotify?

Spotify has more than 138 million users worldwide and each artist's monthly listener count is calculated by how many unique monthly listeners they have. Although, you can see which songs are the most played they can be played many times by a smaller number of people. But the artist with more unique monthly listeners reaches a more wide ranging audience than sometimes the artist with the most songs in the Global Top 50 and you see next to here. It’s the only number shared by Spotify which reflects streams for an artist, rather than for a song. Thus, it’s seen as the perfect shortcut to know who are the most popular artists. And here, we have a game where you can try and guess which artist will come out on top! Try and see how the Global Top 50 compares in the game to the artist with more monthly listeners!

All information is provided from Spotify® with supporting help from Evilarceus's API to collect the data.
